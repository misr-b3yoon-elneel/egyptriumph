About Our Project

Project Vision

To document and showcase Egypt's developmental achievements over the past decade, highlighting the nation's progress in infrastructure, social services, and economic growth.

Our Mission

To create a comprehensive digital platform that educates both Egyptians and international audiences about the transformation Egypt has undergone, using verified data and visual storytelling.

Team Approach

Working under the guidance of our mentor Mr. Ahmed Said, we divided responsibilities based on our strengths:

  • Yassin handled web design and technical implementation
  • Daniel focused on research and content development
  • Both collaborated on data collection and verification
